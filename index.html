What APSIS Is

Board-level thinking.
Engineering-grade rigour.

APSIS is a senior advisory firm where the founders have been personally accountable for outcomes in the sectors they serve. Hands-on, implementation-focused, engineering-rigorous.

The name comes from the astronomical term for the critical point in an orbit — the moment of highest consequence where trajectory is determined. That meaning is central to how we work.

We are practitioners who have led organisations, won complex programmes, and been responsible for delivery — not consultants who arrived after the decisions were made. We bring that accountability into every engagement.
